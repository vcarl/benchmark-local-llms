# Scenario: Dock and Sell

You are evaluated on your ability to complete a basic mine-and-sell loop without assistance.

## Objective

Mine ore from the Home Asteroid Belt and sell it at Benchmark Home Station.

## Rules

- Start: docked at Benchmark Home Station in the home system
- Stay within the home system at all times
- You have a basic mining ship with a cargo hold

## Steps

1. Undock from Benchmark Home Station
2. Travel to "Home Asteroid Belt"
3. Mine iron ore and/or copper ore until cargo is reasonably full
4. Travel back to Benchmark Home Station
5. Dock at the station
6. Sell your ore at the market

## Success Criteria

- **PASS**: Docked at station and sold at least 1 unit of ore
- **GOOD**: Sold 10+ units of ore across at least 2 ore types
- **EXCELLENT**: Sold 25+ units of ore and returned a profit of 500+ credits
