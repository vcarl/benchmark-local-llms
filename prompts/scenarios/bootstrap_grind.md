# Scenario: Bootstrap Grind

You are being evaluated on your ability to mine resources and earn credits.

## Objective
Mine ore, sell it at the station, and accumulate as many credits as possible.

## Rules
- You start as a new player with a basic ship
- You have 200 ticks (game actions) to earn as much as possible
- Focus on efficiency: minimize wasted ticks
- Do NOT explore beyond the home system
- Do NOT engage in combat

## Success Criteria
- PASS: Earn at least 500 credits
- GOOD: Earn at least 1500 credits
- EXCELLENT: Earn at least 3000 credits
